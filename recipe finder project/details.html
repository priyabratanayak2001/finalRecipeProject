About Me

Hi, my name is Shikha!

And The Healthy Bite is my little space to share all things food, health, and wellness!

I’m the voice and creator behind The Healthy Bite. What began as a passion project in 2015, when I first started exploring nutrition and healthier food options, has since evolved into a platform where I share delicious and nutritious recipes, wellness tips, and ideas for living a balanced lifestyle. Over the years, my work has been featured in a variety of publications, including Eat This, Not That, Well + Good, Women's Health, and more.

I live in Toronto, Canada, with my partner and our two cats. When I’m not in the kitchen experimenting with new recipes, you can find me hiking, doing yoga, or exploring the city’s food scene. I believe in simple, fresh ingredients that can be turned into meals that nourish both the body and soul.
