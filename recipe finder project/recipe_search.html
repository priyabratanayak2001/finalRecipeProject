A spicy and flavorful chicken dish perfect for dinner.

Cooking Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices and set aside for 20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions, garlic, and green chilies.

Gobi Aloo Curry

A flavorful and hearty curry made with cauliflower and potatoes in a rich, spiced gravy.

Cooking Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until golden.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, cumin, coriander powder, and cook until tomatoes soften.
  3. Add cauliflower florets and diced potatoes, and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Pour in water, cover, and cook until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt, add garam masala, and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.

Fish Fry Recipe
Step 1
Clean and gut the fish, removing scales and bones as necessary.
Step 2
Marinate the fish with turmeric, salt, pepper, and lemon juice for 20 minutes.
Step 3
Prepare a batter using rice flour, cornstarch, and a pinch of salt. Coat the fish evenly with the batter.
Step 4
Heat oil in a pan over medium heat for frying the fish.
Step 5
Once the oil is hot, gently slide the battered fish into the pan and fry until golden brown on both sides.
Step 6
Remove the fish from the pan and drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.
Step 7
Garnish the fried fish with fresh coriander leaves and slices of onion.
Step 8
Serve the fish fry hot with a side of mint chutney or a tangy dip.
